# Welgo EA Scope — Jessah Babor

## In scope (read + write)

Calls, 2FA codes, physical tasks (mail pickup, courier), simple admin

## Out of scope

Financial decisions, code, comms drafting on behalf of Ed

## Escalate to Ed (post in #opco + tag)

- Any decision outside the in-scope list above
- Any policy question
- Anything you're unsure about

## Rule of thumb

If it falls inside the in-scope list above, it's yours. Anything else, escalate.
